# MVP User Stories

## Dashboard
As a user, I want to see my net worth so I know my progress toward $100,000.

## Emergency Fund
As a user, I want to track my emergency fund against $5,000 and $8,000 targets.

## TFSA
As a user, I want to track TFSA contributions, current value, XEQT holdings, and recurring deposits.

## Dividends
As a user, I want to record dividends and whether they were reinvested through DRIP.

## Reports
As a user, I want a monthly report that tells me what changed and what action to take next.

## CSV Import
As a user, I want to import Wealthsimple, CIBC, and Neo CSVs to reduce manual entry.
