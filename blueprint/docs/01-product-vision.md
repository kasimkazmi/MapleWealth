# 01. Product Vision


Project: FinanceOS Canada, Personal Finance Dashboard
Owner: Master
Version: 1.0
Primary reference: Personal Financial Master Plan (Canada)
Core rule: Emergency Fund -> TFSA -> FHSA -> RRSP. Do not recommend trading, market timing, options, forex, meme stocks, or speculation.


## Vision
Build FinanceOS Canada, a private-first personal finance dashboard that helps the owner grow from current early-stage wealth to $100,000+ net worth and eventually financial independence.

## Problem
Most finance apps are either generic, US-focused, bank-marketing products, or too shallow for serious long-term planning. This product should be Canadian, investment-aware, tax-account-aware, and goal-driven.

## Product Principles
- Source-of-truth planning beats random financial advice.
- Automation beats motivation.
- Long-term ETF investing beats active trading for this user profile.
- Canadian tax-account rules must be first-class, not an afterthought.
- Manual-first import is acceptable for MVP. Banking integrations can come later.

## North Star Metric
Net worth progress toward $100,000 while maintaining emergency fund safety and consistent TFSA investing.

## Non-Goals
- No stock-picking app.
- No day-trading features.
- No crypto-first design.
- No lending or credit product recommendations in MVP.
- No pretending to be a regulated financial advisor.
