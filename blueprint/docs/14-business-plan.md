# 14. Business Plan


Project: FinanceOS Canada, Personal Finance Dashboard
Owner: Master
Version: 1.0
Primary reference: Personal Financial Master Plan (Canada)
Core rule: Emergency Fund -> TFSA -> FHSA -> RRSP. Do not recommend trading, market timing, options, forex, meme stocks, or speculation.


## Positioning
FinanceOS Canada: a Canadian-first personal finance operating system for people who want serious planning, not flashy spending charts.

## Differentiators
- Canadian registered accounts first-class
- TFSA/FHSA/RRSP tracking
- Rules engine based on user goals
- AI review assistant
- Long-term investing philosophy
- Self-hostable/private-first option

## Potential Customers Later
- Newcomers to Canada
- Early-career professionals
- Software developers and tech workers
- Personal finance enthusiasts
- FIRE community

## Monetization Later
- Free self-hosted personal version
- Paid hosted SaaS
- Premium AI reports
- Tax season reports
- Advisor/export package

## Brutal Reality
This should not become public SaaS until the personal version is excellent. Finance apps require trust. Bad calculations destroy credibility instantly.
