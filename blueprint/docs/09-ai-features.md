# 09. AI Features


Project: MapleWealth, Personal Finance Dashboard
Owner: Master
Version: 1.0
Primary reference: Personal Financial Master Plan (Canada)
Core rule: Emergency Fund -> TFSA -> FHSA -> RRSP. Do not recommend trading, market timing, options, forex, meme stocks, or speculation.


## AI Assistant Scope
AI can summarize, explain trade-offs, generate monthly reports, detect anomalies, and suggest next actions. It must not claim to be a licensed financial advisor.

## MVP AI Features
- Monthly financial review
- Spending summary
- Goal progress explanation
- Rule-based warnings in plain English
- CSV import categorization suggestions

## Future AI Features
- Natural language questions over personal finances
- Salary growth planning
- Tax optimization explanations
- Investment allocation explanations
- Scenario planning

## Guardrails
- No personalized securities speculation beyond approved long-term ETF strategy.
- No market timing advice.
- No high-risk product encouragement.
- Always include assumptions.
- Ask for missing numbers before making major projections.
